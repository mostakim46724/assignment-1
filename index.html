Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Infrastructure

The concept of Bangladesh 2.0 envisions the transformation of Bangladesh into a more developed and technologically advanced nation. Infrastructure plays a critical role in this transformation. Here are the key pillars of infrastructure in the Bangladesh 2.0 vision:Broadband Connectivity,Data Centers & Cloud Services,Smart Cities

Sustainability

The sustainability aspect of Bangladesh 2.0 is crucial to ensuring long-term growth and development while preserving natural resources, reducing environmental impact, and improving social welfare. The sustainability pillars of Bangladesh 2.0 focus on creating a balance between economic growth, environmental protection, and social inclusion. Here are the main pillars of sustainability for Bangladesh 2.0:Renewable Energy, Green Infrastructure,Forest Conservation and Reforestation.

Infrastructure

The infrastructure pillars of Bangladesh 2.0 are designed to foster a transformative shift toward a more advanced and sustainable nation. These pillars focus on strengthening the nation’s physical, digital, and social infrastructure to support long-term economic growth, technological innovation, and improved living standards. The key infrastructure pillars of Bangladesh 2.0 include:Broadband and Internet Access,Data Centers and Cloud Computing,Smart Cities,Cybersecurity Infrastructure

Empowerment

The empowerment pillars of Bangladesh 2.0 focus on creating an inclusive society where every citizen, especially women, youth, and marginalized communities, has the opportunity to thrive. These pillars emphasize equal access to opportunities, resources, and participation in economic, social, and political spheres. Empowerment is key to realizing the vision of a prosperous, equitable, and sustainable Bangladesh.

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in
renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly
future.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global
competitiveness.

The concept of "2.0" associated with Dr. Yunus revolves around a transformative economic model he champions, known as Social Business. The idea focuses on reshaping traditional capitalism by emphasizing social impact over profit maximization. Below are some core aspects of this vision:

  • Purpose over Profit
  • Self-Sustainability
  • Empowerment through Innovation
